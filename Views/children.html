Children Ministry

The mission of the Children’s Ministry is to help children worship God. The ministry strives to create a secure and safe environment that is attentive, engaging, and encouraging to each child who is a part of the ministry. This ministry engages kids in lessons that lead to a better understanding of Jesus. The ministry hosts Children’s Church during the worship service on Sundays

Headed by Sister Gloria

The Children Ministry is focused on making kids grow in their relationship with Jesus. The watchword for this ministry is taking from the bible that instructs us to “Train up a child in the way he should go: and when he is old, he will not depart from it” Proverbs 22:6
The Children Ministry is divided into 5 classes covering the ages of 1 to 14 years.

Beginners Class

Ages 1-4 Years

The beginner’s class helps kids learn about Jesus from an early age. The class is focused on building the foundation of these kids, and this guides them through various stages of life. This class is headed by Sister Odun.

Friends of God

Ages 5-6 Years

The Friends of God’s class is focused on kids that are just starting Kindergarten class. Kids at this age mix with children from other religions, and will learn different views about God. This class is aimed at helping these kids find the truth about God and hold on to that which is true; from the foundation of their lives. This class is headed by Sis. Idy.

Adventures Class

Ages 7-8 Years

Kids between these age are bundles of energy and ready to explore all the knowledge and practices they have seen or heard about. The Adventurer’s Sunday School class redirects the energy of these kids on Godly activities that bring glory to the name of the Lord. This class is headed by Sis. Abiola.

Pilot Class

Ages 9-11 Years

The Pilot class has helps children develop their own sense of right and wrong. The Teacher advices the kids against risky things friends might pressure them to do, like smoking or dangerous physical dares. The children are constantly reminded of God love for them and his commandments pertaining to different areas of life. This class is headed by Sis. Gloria.

Teenage Class

Ages 12-14 Years

The teenagers’ class is about the last opportunity for the Sunday school teachers to impact positively into the foundation of the children before they graduate into youth Sunday school. At this age, teens make more of their own choices about friends, sports, studying, and school. They become more independent, with their own personality and interests, although parents are still very important. The Teachers help the teen make healthy Godly choices while encouraging them to make their own decisions. This class is headed by Brother Charles.
