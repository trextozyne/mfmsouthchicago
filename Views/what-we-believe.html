What We Believe

  1. The Bible - The Scriptures are the inspired Word of God; the only basis for our faith and fellowship.
  2. Holy Spirit in the Trinity - The One True God, ETERNAL EXISTENT in three Persons: God the Father, God the Son, and the Holy Spirit.
  3. Universal Redemption of Mankind - The fall and deprivation of mankind, necessitating redemption through the Blood of Jesus Christ.
  4. Salvation of Our Soul - The Salvation of Mankind is through the redeeming work of Jesus Christ and the regenerative work of the Holy Spirit.
  5. Sanctification from sin - Sanctification is seen as an act of separation from that which is evil.
  6. Baptism is Important - The Baptism of the Holy Spirit – Acts 2:4; 10:44 and 19:1-6
  7. Restitution Leads to Restoration -  We believe in Restitution for past wrongs where possible
  8. We Practice Ordinance - The Ordinance of the Church, e.g. the Lord’s Supper and Water Baptism
  9. Church Universal - The visible church is the outward structure and appearance of those who claim to be Christians. The invisible church consists of those were truly born again and are new creatures in Christ (2 Corinthians 5:17).
  10. Ministry - The Ministry, divinely called and scripturally ordained as the approved of Almighty God
  11. Healing - Divine healing as provided by the Lord Jesus Christ The Rapture ushering all believers into the Marriage Supper of the Lamb, and the second advent of the Lord Jesus Christ when He will physically land on the earth
  12. The Millennial Reign of Christ
  13. The Final Judgment
  14. The New Heaven and Earth
